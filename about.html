About us

What is Jewellery-In ?

We are a wholesale supplier of beautifully designed, gorgeously affordable fashion jewellery. Our business was started in 1999 as a retail outlet selling gifts and jewellery for life’s special occasions. In 2005 we decided to launch ask jewellery. We started with one agent selling to shops in one county. Since then our range has evolved and our business has grown . We now supply some of the UK’s most successful independent retailers.

jewellery-In is…

  • Stylish & classically on trend.
  • Original in design.
  • Easy to wear. Within the range there are pieces to appeal to women of all ages and for most occasions.
  • Mostly handmade and entirely finished by hand.
  • Enhanced by the inclusion of freshwater pearls and semi- precious stones such as amethyst, jade, agate and rose quartz. This gives ask jewellery an intrinsic value and a timeless appeal that sets it apart
  • Made by talented people who are themselves small family companies.

If you would like to find out more about jewellery-In, please contact us. We would be delighted to hear from you.
